Chagos Handover Shelved After Trump Withdraws Support

The UK–Mauritius deal to hand over the Chagos Islands has been shelved after Trump withdrew US support. The agreement, negotiated under Biden, would have transferred sovereignty while retaining the Diego Garcia military base. Trump’s opposition leaves the deal in limbo and raises questions about UK sovereignty decisions made under American pressure.

Stage for Freedom — 10,000 March to US Embassy

A major demonstration in London saw 10,000 people march from Whitehall to the US Embassy. The protest focused on civil liberties, anti-war messaging, and opposition to emergency powers used during the fuel crisis. A heavy police security operation was in place. The march follows the 500,000-strong Together Alliance rally on 29 March.

Junior Doctor Strike — Final Full Day Before Monday Return

The six-day walkout enters its final full day — day five of consultant-only cover across England. Costs have exceeded £250m. The Government’s withdrawal of 1,000 training posts stands. The strike ends at 6:59am Monday, the same morning Parliament returns. Health Secretary urges the BMA to negotiate.

Grenfell Memorial Bill Advances — Lords Second Reading Monday

The Grenfell Memorial Bill, authorising a permanent memorial, archive, and exhibition at the tower site, advances to its Lords second reading on Monday.
