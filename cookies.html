Cookie Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. This policy also covers localStorage, a similar browser technology that allows websites to store data locally on your device. Unlike traditional cookies, localStorage data is not sent to the server with each request — it stays entirely on your device.

2. Technologies We Use

The Daily Brief uses localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store the following data on your device:

Key Type Purpose Duration
theme Essential Remembers your light/dark mode preference Persistent
notifySubscribed Essential Tracks whether you have enabled edition notifications Persistent
db_bookmarks Essential Stores your saved/bookmarked stories Persistent
db_markets Performance Caches market data to reduce API calls and improve load times Persistent
db_cookie_consent Essential Records that you have accepted this cookie policy Persistent
db_subscribe_email Functional Stores your email address if you express interest in premium subscriptions Persistent

All items marked "Persistent" remain stored until you manually clear your browser data.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may set its own cookies according to Google's privacy policy. We do not use any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies.

4. Future Analytics

If we introduce analytics or tracking technologies in the future, this policy will be updated before any such change takes effect. We will request your explicit consent before setting any non-essential cookies or trackers.

5. Managing and Clearing Stored Data

You can view and delete localStorage data through your browser's developer tools, or clear all site data through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → select "Cookies and other site data"
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage Data
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Note: clearing site data will reset your preferences (theme, bookmarks, notification status) and you will need to set them again.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. We will notify you of material changes via a notice on the Site.

For questions about this policy, please contact thedailybriefuk@gmail.com.
